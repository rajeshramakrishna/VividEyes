Who We Are

Adityavani Facility Services (AVFS) is one of the leading facility management services in Bengaluru that houses over 600 employees. We add value to our customers’ organizations allowing for increased focus on core competences by unleashing the potential of outsourcing.

We utilize latest technology and apply high standards of quality for our services. A single solution is provided by combining many services, and this is done to ensure that one service can support another. By bundling services, we are able to reduce overheads and allocate resources more efficiently. For example, reception services can easily be combined with administrative support, with an employee easily switching roles from the front desk to a guard. This synergy comes from our ability to integrate services and optimize delivery systems.

Why choose AVFS

At AVFS, we deliver a wide range of self-performed services that can be combined in numerous ways according to a customer’s needs. Creating a flexible, transparent and tailor-made facility management solution in close dialogue with the customer is critical for scaling services to meet the changing needs of the organisation.
